Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation is when an individual or groups of individuals are given authority to take control over their lives and make their own decisions. This includes access to education, economic independence, and the ability to participate in social, economic, and political activities. Empowerment is the practice of motivating yourself or others to accomplish important goals or objectives. It helps enable people to take initiative, make decisions for themselves and solve complex problems in an effort to improve their lives.
